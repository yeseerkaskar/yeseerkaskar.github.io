I'm a Master of Information Technology & Systems student at the University of Tasmania, specialising in Data Analytics and AI, with a Computer Engineering degree and an IEEE paper behind me.

I like the whole pipeline — framing the problem, wrangling the data, training the model, and shipping the interface people actually use. That means I'm as comfortable comparing ML classifiers as I am building a Laravel app or a React dashboard.
